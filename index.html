About the Scam Link Detector

The Scam Link Detector is designed to help you verify the safety of URLs before you click on them. With the rise of phishing scams and malicious links, this tool uses advanced algorithms and trusted domain lists to identify suspicious patterns. Simply enter a URL, and the tool will quickly analyze it to determine if it's legitimate or potentially harmful.

Our detection system checks for common indicators of scam links, such as:

  • Misleading keywords like "free," "prize," or "urgent."
  • Shortened URLs or unknown domains.
  • Suspicious file types and encoded characters.

Stay safe online by verifying links before sharing your personal information. If a link is flagged, avoid clicking on it and report it to the relevant authorities.
